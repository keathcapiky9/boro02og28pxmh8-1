<p><p>The clove powder market can be segmented into two main types: whole grain clove and crumbs. Whole grain clove consists of intact, dried clove buds, offering a robust flavor and higher potency, often used in gourmet cooking and baking. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for flavor intensity and application in food preparation.</p></p>
