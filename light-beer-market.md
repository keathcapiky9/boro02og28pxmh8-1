<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Light Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Limit Fermentation</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light beer market can be categorized into two primary types based on fermentation methods: limit fermentation and dealcoholization. Limit fermentation involves reducing the yeast's activity, resulting in lower alcohol content while maintaining flavor, appealing to health-conscious consumers. In contrast, the dealcoholization method removes alcohol from fully fermented beer, preserving most of the original taste. Both methods aim to create lower-calorie, lower-alcohol beverages, catering to increasing demand for lighter drinking options without compromising on flavor.</p></p>
