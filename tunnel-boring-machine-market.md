<p><strong>The Tunnel Boring Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Ground TBMs</li><li>Hard Rock TBMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are categorized into two main types based on the geological conditions they operate in: Soft Ground TBMs and Hard Rock TBMs. Soft Ground TBMs are designed to excavate through materials like clay, silt, and sand, utilizing a shield to support the surrounding soil. In contrast, Hard Rock TBMs are built to tackle solid geological formations, employing powerful cutting tools to break through dense rock. Both types play essential roles in infrastructure development and underground construction projects.</p></p>
