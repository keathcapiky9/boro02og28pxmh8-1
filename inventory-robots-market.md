<p><p>The inventory robots market consists of two primary types: mobile robotics and fixed robotics. Mobile robotics entails autonomous robots that navigate through warehouses or retail spaces, optimizing inventory management by transporting goods and conducting stock takes. In contrast, fixed robotics refers to stationary systems such as automated conveyor belts or robotic arms used for tasks like sorting and shelving in designated areas. Both types enhance operational efficiency, reduce human labor costs, and improve inventory accuracy across various industries.</p></p>
